Colin Greenwood

THOM  JONNY  COLIN  ED  PHIL 

Colin Charles Greenwood (born 26 June 1969), also known as Coz, is a member of English rock band Radiohead. He is best known as their bass player, although he does play other instruments (see below). He is the older brother of fellow band member, guitarist Jonny Greenwood.
