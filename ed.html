Ed O'Brien

THOM  JONNY  COLIN  ED  PHIL 

Edward John O'Brien (born 15 April 1968, in Oxford, England) is a member of Radiohead. He plays guitar and is responsible for harmony vocals during live concerts and on many tracks from the band's albums. He was ranked number 60 on Rolling Stone's 100 Greatest Guitarists of All Time
